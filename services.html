Boat Tours

Experience the open water and witness secret coves, islands, and unique history. Join us as we detail the history of the unique region as well as first hand accounts of events that have transpired. For an extra cost, experience all 5 Great Lakes as we take you from Duluth, MN to Rochester, NY.

Fishing

Experience the lifestyle that is fishing by going on a guided tour with one of our many professional fishermen. See how this hobby, lifestyle, and way of life is done in the North American Great Lakes Region.

Times vary from 1 hour to half a day to sunrise to sunset. You decide how long you want to spend out in the open lakes with yourself, your thoughts, and your pole.

Island Hopping

From The Apostle Islands, Beaver Island, Washington Island, or even Isle Royale, come with us as we explore many of the different islands in the Great lakes. Depending on the service style that you chose, this expedition can last from 2 hours to 12 hours. At each island we will stop and learn the unique history as well as its biodiversity and climate.
